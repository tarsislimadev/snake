snake

F. A. Q.

How do I play the game?
Just use the arrow keys to move the Snake. The goal is to eat the food that appears on the screen.
What happens if the snake hits the wall or itself?
The game ends. The game will restart. Try again to beat your previous score!
How is the score calculated?
Every piece of food you eat adds points to your score.
Can I play offline?
Yes, the game works without an internet connection, but your score will not be saved.
